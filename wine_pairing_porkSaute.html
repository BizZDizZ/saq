Pork sauté

Preparation

  1. Thinly slice the pork tenderloins.
  2. In a bowl, mix together all the marinade ingredients.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours.
  4. Remove the meat from the marinade
  5. Sear the pieces of pork 2 or 3 times to brown well. Set aside on a plate.
  6. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, reduce the marinade over medium heat until syrupy.
  7. In the wok, brown the mushrooms and green onions over medium-high heat.
  8. Add 15 ml (1 tbsp) of ginger.
  9. Serve the pork on a bed of basmati or jasmine rice, with broccoli or another green vegetable.
  • Preparation 20 minutes
  • Cooking time 15 minutes
  • Servings 4
