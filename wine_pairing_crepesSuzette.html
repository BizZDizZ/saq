Crepes Suzette

Preparation

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the sugar and cook for about 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar and salt.
  4. Make a well in the centre and add the eggs.
  5. With a whisk, combine the eggs with the flour. Add 1/3 of the milk and stir vigorously until well combined with no lumps remaining.
  6. Gradually add the rest of the milk while stirring constantly.
  7. In a skillet, heat a few drops of oil over medium-high heat. (You may oil the skillet, rubbing with a cloth or paper towel moistened with oil.)
  8. When the syrup starts to boil, heat 6 crepes for about 1 minute, turning them over 2 or 3 times. Pour in 15 ml (1 tbsp) of orange liqueur and flambé.
  9. Top with the hot syrup and garnish with orange zest.
  10. Repeat the same steps for the remaining 6 crepes.
  • Preparation 20 minutes
  • Cooking time 30 minutes
  • Servings 4
