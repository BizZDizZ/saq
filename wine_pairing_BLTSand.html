Espace Québec Pairing

BLT sandwiches

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 80°C (175°F).
  2. Cut the grape tomatoes in half, lengthwise.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the salt, sugar and rosemary. Add some ground pepper.
  4. Approximately 30 minutes before the end of the tomatoes' cooking time, place the slices of pancetta on a baking sheet.
  5. Place in the oven to warm for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove the tomatoes from the oven and set aside to cool for 15 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, in a bowl, mix together the mayonnaise and pesto.
  8. Toast the slices of bread in a toaster. Rub each slice of bread with the cut side of garlic.
  9. Spread some pesto mayonnaise on the slices of bread.
  10. Make the sandwich, layering first some arugula, then the dried tomatoes and finally the pancetta.
  • Preparation 15 minutes
  • Cooking time 2 hours
  • Servings 4
