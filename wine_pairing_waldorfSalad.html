Waldorf Salad

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the apples, raisins, green onions, celery and walnuts.
  2. Thicken with the mayonnaise.
  3. Add the lemon juice and yogurt. Stir.
  4. Set aside and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  5. Place on a bed of lettuce.
  • Preparation 15 minutes
  • Cooking time none
  • Servings 4
