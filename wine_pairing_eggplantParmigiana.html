Eggplant parmigiana

Preparation

  1. Generously sprinkle the eggplant rounds with coarse salt.
  2. Let sit for 20 minutes to allow excess liquid to drain.
  3. Heat the tomato sauce, then add the tomato paste and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Rinse the eggplant rounds well and pat dry with paper towels.
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet. Flour and lightly season the eggplant rounds with pepper.
  6. Cook on both sides until they turn light brown. Remove from skillet and reserve.
  7. Coat the bottom of a 5 cm (2 in.) deep oven-safe dish with a bit of tomato sauce, then cover with the eggplant rounds.
  8. Coat with more tomato sauce, sprinkle with the Parmesan and top with the mozzarella.
  • Preparation 15 minutes
  • Cooking time 20 to 25 minutes
  • Servings 4
