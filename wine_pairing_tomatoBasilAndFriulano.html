Espace Québec Pairing

Tomato, basil and Friulano

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a bowl, carefully mix tomatoes, basil, oil and vinegar.
  3. Season generously.
  4. Place bread slices on a baking sheet. Broil 1-2 minutes.
  5. Immediately after removing bread from the oven, rub them with the halved garlic cloves.
  6. Add cubes of cheese to the tomato mixture.
  7. Top bread slices with this mix.
  8. Serve immediately.
  • Preparation 15 minutes
  • Cooking time 2 minutes
  • Servings 4
