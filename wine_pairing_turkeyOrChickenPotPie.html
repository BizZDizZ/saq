Espace Québec Pairing

Turkey or chicken pot pie

Preparation

  1. In a skillet, melt 30 ml (2 tbsp) of butter over low heat
  2. In a saucepan, melt the rest of the butter over low heat, and add the flour.
  3. Cook over low heat without colouring. Stir the roux from time to time.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth while stirring to prevent lumps in the velouté.
  5. Meanwhile, mix together the egg and milk in a small bowl. Set aside.
  6. Add the cooked chicken and vegetables to the velouté. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Pour the mixture into a 23 to 25 cm (9 to 10 in.) ovenproof pan.
  8. Place the pastry shell or dough over the mixture (dough must be 1 cm (1/2 in.) larger than the pan).
  9. Make a slit in the centre of the dough. Brush the top with the egg wash.
  10. Bake in the oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until the top of the pie is golden brown.
  • Preparation 25 to 30 minutes
  • Cooking time 40 minutes
  • Servings 4 to 5
